Short bio
After a master in Physics and a Ph.D. in Environmental Sciences (AgroParisTech/UPMC), I occupied several post-doctoral position in France, Italy and the US. I am currently working as a Researcher at Observatoire Midi-Pyrénées (Toulouse, France). For several years, I have also worked as a permanent Research Scientist at the Abdus Salam International Center for Theoretical Physics (UNESCO, Trieste, Italy) for which I am still an active collaborator. My main field of expertise relates to atmospheric physics and chemistry (including some biogeochemical aspects), regional climate modelling, and impact assessments. I use mostly modeling and data analytics approaches.
